Goa


Goa is a beautiful coastal destination in India known for its stunning beaches, vibrant nightlife, rich Portuguese heritage, and relaxing tropical vibe.

Top 5 Attractions

  1. Baga Beach – Famous for nightlife and water sports
  2. Fort Aguada – Historic fort with scenic sea views
  3. Dudhsagar Waterfalls – One of the tallest waterfalls in India
  4. Basilica of Bom Jesus – UNESCO World Heritage Site
  5. Anjuna Beach – Popular for flea markets and sunsets

Must-Try Foods

  • Goan Fish Curry
  • Prawn Balchão
  • Bebinca
  • Pork Vindaloo
  • Feni
