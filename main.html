Thailand


To strike out on your own, head to this semihidden beach on the island’s far southern shore. Accessible via a 3km walk down a steep hill (or a brief, bumpy ride in the back of the truck), the petite cove is attractively framed by palm trees and massive boulders. You’ll find a handful of low-key bars and restaurants, and although you won’t be alone, you almost certainly won’t feel like part of the crowd.

Top Attractions

  1. Beach - Relax and enjoy the sun.
  2. Local Markets - Experience the vibrant culture.
  3. Diving Spots - Explore the underwater beauty.
  4. Historical Temples - Discover the rich history.
  5. Nightlife - Enjoy the lively atmosphere.

Popular Foods

  • Pizza - A must-try with a local twist.
  • Egg Roll - A delicious snack.
  • Noodles - Savory and flavorful.
  • Yum - A popular Thai dish.
